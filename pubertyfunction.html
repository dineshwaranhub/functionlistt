In Tamil Nadu, the puberty function, commonly referred to as "Puthir Kalyanam" or "Manjal
Neerattu Vizha," is a traditional ceremony celebrated to mark a girl's attainment of puberty.
This event holds significant cultural and familial importance and is steeped in Tamil customs.
