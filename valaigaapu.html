Valagappu is a traditional Tamil Nadu celebration held to honor and celebrate
a pregnant woman's journey towards motherhood. It's a Tamil custom that takes place during the last
few months of pregnancy. The event marks the transition into
motherhood and is a way for the family and friends to wish the expectant mother good health and happiness.
